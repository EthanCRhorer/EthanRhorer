General Information About Website

Educate local liberals about the importance of local politics and where they might fight into it. Let them figure out which group they fall in, depending on their ideology. Many voters instinctively say they are hyper-progressive as that is what is in vogue in our community, so I will give prompts that does not tell the user which side is progressive and which side is progressive. This will ensure an accurate assessment of the user’s best political fit.

Bay Area voters, specifically liberal Bay Area voters who do not have a local political identity.

  • Since the purpose is to give people a local political identity, making the questions as non-partisan as possible is key to having people leave their biases behind
  • Since the topic is charged, the product should be abstract, and not hot button issues, in order to cut through partisanship.
  • Since the product is more or less a quiz, having a tracker of how far people are through the quiz is important to inform the user of how many more questions they have to answer.
  • Since the audience is mostly liberal, the prompts should be framed around disputes within liberal circles, not around liberal vs. conservative groups. This is to sub-define people’s politics past their national identity.
